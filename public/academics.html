Academic Structure

Madrasat-ul-Bayt Institute provides a comprehensive academic program rooted in both national education standards and traditional Islamic teachings. The school offers the following stages of learning:

  • Primary/Preparatory School (Ages 6–12)
  • Secondary/High School (Ages 12–17)
  • College of Islamic & Vocational Studies (Ages 16–21) — Starting September 2025

Curriculum Breakdown

Primary/Preparatory School

  • National Standards Curriculum (NSC): Language Arts, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies
  • Akhlaaq, Arabic & Islamic Studies
  • Qur’an Memorization

Secondary/High School

  • CSEC Subjects: English, Math, Spanish, Principles of Business & Accounts, Food & Nutrition, HSB, Chemistry, IT, Social Studies
  • Akhlaaq Intelligence: Akhlaaq, Tafseer, Hadith, Aqeedah, Seerah, Islamic Studies
  • Arabic & Qur’an Memorization

College of Islamic & Vocational Studies

  • Advanced Islamic Studies Certificate
  • Madinah Arabic, Qur’an Memorization & Translation
  • Skills: Web Design, Woodwork, Glass Fabrication, Electrical, Accounting, Construction

The program is designed to raise Islamic workers grounded in Qur’an and Sunnah, who can serve and uplift their communities with knowledge and entrepreneurship.
