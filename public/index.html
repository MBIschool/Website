About Us

Madrasat-ul-Bayt Institute (MBI) was established in August 2013 when a small group of families committed themselves to raising upright and grounded children in Islam. What began as a homeschool effort soon grew, and by August 2015, the institution was officially registered. Rooted in the Quran and Sunnah, MBI has consistently produced students with strong character (Akhlaaq) and solid academic foundations.

From its humble beginnings in family homes, MBI continues to evolve with an expanding student body and educational scope, all while staying true to its mission of fostering Islamic values and lifelong learning.

Academics & Courses

MBI offers a well-rounded academic experience across three educational stages:

  • Primary/Preparatory (Ages 6–12): Follows the National Standards Curriculum (NSC) including Language Arts, Math, Science, and Social Studies, along with Akhlaaq, Arabic, Islamic Studies, and Quran memorization.
  • Secondary/High School (Ages 12–17): Offers the CSEC syllabus in English, Math, Science, Business, IT, and Social Studies, plus advanced Islamic studies in Tafseer, Hadith, Aqeedah, Seerah, and Quran memorization.
  • College of Islamic & Vocational Studies (Ages 16–21): Launching in September 2025, this program focuses on Islamic training and vocational skills such as web design, woodwork, glass fabrication, electrical fundamentals, and construction basics.

These programs are designed to cultivate strong Islamic character while equipping students with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in modern society.

Admissions

MBI welcomes students from diverse backgrounds who are eager to grow spiritually and academically. The admissions process includes:

  • Eligibility: Students ages 6–21 interested in a comprehensive Islamic and academic education.
  • Application Process: Submit an online or paper application including personal details, academic history, and required documents.
  • Required Documents: Birth certificate, previous school reports (if applicable), and identification documents.
  • Tuition & Fees: Structured per educational level. Details available upon request.
  • Scholarships: Limited financial aid may be available for deserving students. Contact the school office for more information.

At MBI, we strive to make the admission process smooth and welcoming. Reach out to our team for assistance or begin the process via our Apply Now page.
